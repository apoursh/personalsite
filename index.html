About:


I am a fourth year graduate student in the physics department at Stanford University. My PhD advisor is Prof. Carlos D. Bustamante in the department of biomedical data sciences. My department of physics co-advisor is Prof. Steven Chu. My primary research interest is developing approaches for performing decentralized GWAS. I am also interested in modeling biological systems using the tools of network analysis. See my research page for a detailed list of my projects.
Prior to Stanford, I studied physics and mathematics at Harvard University and had the opportunity to do research with Prof. Isaac F. Silvera in high-pressure physics and, Prof. Abhijit Majumder in theoretical high-energy physics.
